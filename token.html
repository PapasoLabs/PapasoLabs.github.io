Note: you do not need to acquire IRON to interact with its service. Once the project is implemented on Ethereum main network, you would use our infrastructure to set decentralized agreements for free, using Ethereum (ETH) as currency for those transactions. Although, every time a transaction is made using our smart contracts, a contribute fee will apply. You can learn more about this here. These collected funds will be automatically distributed between all IRON token holders, earning ETH in the process.

Note: the gas price (gwei) sent with your transaction must be equal or higher than the current Recommended Gas Price (SafeLow). Otherwise, your transaction will take long to be attached into the Ethereum blockchain, or even fail, as the miners will not be encouraged to mine yours.
